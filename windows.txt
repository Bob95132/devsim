devsim.zip

This is the DEVSIM distribution in a zip format.  Extract the files from
the archive into your preferred directory, e.g. c:\.  Product
documentation is available in the devsim\doc directory.

DEVSIM also depends on ActiveTcl 8.5.15.0 and ActivePython 2.7.8.10.
They are available at:
http://www.activestate.com

You may need to install the Visual C++ 2013 Redistributable package for
your architecture.  It is available from here:

http://www.microsoft.com/en-us/download/details.aspx?id=40784

It contains system libraries required for running programs compiled in
Visual C++.  This package may already installed
be installed on your system.

Note that your PATH should have the following paths:
C:\Python27;C:\Tcl\bin;C:\devsim

Many of the examples require your PYTHONPATH to contain the DEVSIM directory:
C:\Python27\Lib;C:\devsim
